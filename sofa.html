Professional Sofa Deep Cleaning

At SIDRA, we specialize in **Sofa Deep Cleaning** services designed to restore the beauty, comfort, and hygiene of your furniture. Our expert cleaners use advanced techniques and safe, eco-friendly products to remove dirt, stains, and allergens from all types of upholstery.

Over time, sofas accumulate dust, body oils, food particles, and microbes that can affect their appearance and freshness. Regular deep cleaning not only improves the look but also extends the lifespan of your furniture.

Our process includes thorough vacuuming, spot treatment for stains, steam cleaning or shampooing, and fabric protection. For leather sofas, we use special conditioners to restore softness and shine without damaging the material.

We handle all types of upholstery including fabric, leather, suede, and microfiber. Our team ensures every inch of your sofa — armrests, cushions, backrest, and corners — is cleaned and sanitized.

Our cleaning solutions are child-safe, pet-friendly, and free from harsh chemicals, leaving your home with a fresh, pleasant fragrance.

Whether it’s a single-seater, L-shaped, or recliner sofa, we guarantee a spotless and refreshing finish. Experience the SIDRA difference today.
